Hover Mask Reveal - Property Controls

Preview
Toggles the liquid mask effect in the Framer canvas. On: effect visible; Off: static. Default: On.

Front
Base image shown by default (responsive image). Required for the effect.

Back
Image revealed on hover/interaction (responsive image). Swaps with Front via the liquid mask.

Parallax
Enables mouse-based parallax movement of the effect. Default: On.

Amount
Parallax movement distance in pixels when Parallax is On. Range: 0–100 px. Default: 100 px. Hidden when Parallax is Off.

Smoothing
Smoothing of the parallax motion (0 = snappy, 1 = very smooth). Range: 0–1. Default: 0. Hidden when Parallax is Off.

Radius
Border radius of the component. Range: 0–100 px. Default: 0.

Size
Size of the liquid “splat” on interaction. Range: 0.02–0.2. Default: 0.08.

Strength
Strength of the circular reveal. Range: 0.2–1. Default: 0.6.

Return time
Time in seconds for the mask to shrink back after interaction. Range: 0.5–10 s. Default: 2.4 s.

Edge grain
Amount of texture/grain on the liquid edge. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.7.

Swirl
Curl/swirl intensity of the liquid. Range: 0–100. Default: 30.
