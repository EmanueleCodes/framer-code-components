Noisy Ripple Reveal - Property Controls

Preview
When On, the overlay gradient animates subtly in the Framer canvas. When Off, a static gradient is shown. On / Off.

Masked
When On, the overlay is fully visible (content hidden). When Off, the component animates to reveal the content from the center out with a noisy ripple edge. On / Off.

Duration
Length of the reveal animation when switching to Unmasked. Range: 0.3–4 s. Default: 1.5 s.

Image
Image to reveal. When Masked is On, the noisy gradient covers it; when Masked is Off, the ripple animates from the center to reveal this image.

Top
Overlay gradient color at top. Default: #e6664d.

Left
Overlay gradient color at bottom-left. Default: #331a99.

Right
Overlay gradient color at bottom-right. Default: #00cccc. More components at [Framer University](https://frameruni.link/cc).
