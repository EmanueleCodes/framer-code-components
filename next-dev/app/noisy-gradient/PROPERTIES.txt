Noisy Ripple Reveal - Property Controls

Preview
Whether the effect runs in the Framer canvas. On / Off.

Masked
Whether the image starts masked (reveal on uncheck). When Off, only the image is visible with a transparent background.

Transition
Duration, easing, and delay for the reveal animation (same as other Framer transition controls).

Image
Source image to reveal.

Shader
Fine-tune shader parameters. Sub-controls:
  Octaves – Noise cart octaves (1–4).
  Warp – Warped distance noise cart (0–2).
  Freq – Wave frequency (4–60).
  Displace – How much the image is distorted by the wave (0–1).
  Chromatic – Chromatic aberration strength (0–0.2).
  Intensity – Glow intensity (color dodge at wavefront). 0–1.
  Edge – Additive white at the wave edge. 0–0.5. More components at [Framer University](https://frameruni.link/cc).
