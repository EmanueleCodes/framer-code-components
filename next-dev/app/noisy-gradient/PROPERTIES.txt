Noisy Gradient - Property Controls

Preview
When On, the gradient animation runs in the Framer canvas. When Off, a static frame is shown to improve editor performance. On / Off.

Speed
Animation speed of the gradient wave. Range: 0–3. Default: 1.

Top
Color at the top of the gradient (peach in the original shader). Default: #e6664d.

Left
Color at the bottom-left (purple in the original). Default: #331a99.

Right
Color at the bottom-right (teal in the original). Default: #00cccc. More components at [Framer University](https://frameruni.link/cc).
