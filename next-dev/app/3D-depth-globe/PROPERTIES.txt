Depth Globe - Property Controls

Detail
Resolution of the globe points. Lower = faster load, less detail. Options: Low (~300k), Medium (~600k), High (~1.2M). Default: Medium.

Preview
When enabled, the globe animation runs in the Framer canvas. When disabled, the globe is frozen to improve editor performance. On / Off.

Globe
Settings for the globe mesh and motion.
- Scale: Overall scale of the globe in the frame. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.9.
- Depth: How exaggerated the terrain height is (elevation scale). Higher values make mountains stand out more. Range: 0.01–0.5. Default: 0.3.
- Pixels: When On, elevation points animate in with a subtle wobble. When Off, the globe appears in its final state.
- Spin: When On, the globe slowly rotates. When Off, you can still drag to orbit.
- Speed: Speed of auto-rotation when Spin is On. Range: 0.1–2. Default: 0.3. Hidden when Spin is Off.
- Dots Size: Size of the terrain points. Range: 0.3–2.5. Default: 1.
- Softness: Edge softness of the points. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.5.
- Smoothing: Smoothing of the terrain. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.6.
- Quality: Render resolution multiplier (e.g. 2 = sharper, heavier). Range: 0.5–3. Default: 1.

Colors
Scene and surface colors.
- Background: Background color behind the globe. Optional; leave unset for transparent (disables glow).
- Land: Color of the elevated land (terrain) points. Default: #fff0d1.
- Water: Color of the ocean and inner water sphere. Default: #0d111a.
- Blend: How much the water color blends into the land at low elevations. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.96.
- Opacity: Opacity of the inner water sphere. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.81.

Glow
Bloom / glow effect.
- Radius: Size of the bloom glow around bright areas. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.48.
- Strength: Intensity of the bloom. Range: 0–2. Default: 0.6.
- Threshold: Brightness above which bloom is applied. Lower = more of the scene blooms. Range: 0–1. Default: 0.

Light
Lighting and exposure.
- Color: Color of the directional and ambient light. Default: #ffd0b8.
- Intensity: Brightness of the scene lighting. Range: 0–2. Default: 0.9.
- Exposure: Overall exposure of the rendered image. Range: 0.1–2. Default: 1. More components at Framer University.
