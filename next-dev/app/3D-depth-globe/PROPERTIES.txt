Depth Globe - Property Controls

Preview
When enabled, the globe animation runs in the Framer canvas. When disabled, the globe is frozen to improve editor performance.

Globe data URL
URL to a JSON file containing the globe elevation data. The JSON must have a "points" array; each point is [latitude, longitude, elevation, land] where land is 0 or 1. Host your own file or use a provided sample URL.

Animate
When enabled, the globe’s elevation points animate in with a subtle wobble. When disabled, the globe appears in its final state.

Auto rotate
When enabled, the globe slowly rotates. When disabled, you can still drag to orbit the camera.

Rotate speed
Speed of auto-rotation. Higher values rotate the globe faster.

Land color
Color of the elevated land (terrain) points on the globe.

Water color
Color of the ocean and low-lying areas, and the inner water sphere.

Land/water blend
How much the water color blends into the land color at low elevations. Higher values make coastlines blend more with the water color.

Elevation scale
How exaggerated the terrain height is. Higher values make mountains stand out more.

Water opacity
Opacity of the inner water sphere (ocean surface). Lower values make it more transparent.

Background
Background color of the scene behind the globe.

Bloom radius
Size of the bloom glow around bright areas. Higher values create a softer, wider glow.

Bloom strength
Intensity of the bloom effect. Higher values make the globe appear brighter and more glowing.

Bloom threshold
Brightness level above which bloom is applied. Lower values make more of the scene bloom.

Light color
Color of the directional and ambient light illuminating the globe.

Light intensity
Brightness of the scene lighting. Higher values make the globe brighter.

Exposure
Overall exposure (brightness) of the rendered image. Adjust if the globe looks too dark or too bright.
